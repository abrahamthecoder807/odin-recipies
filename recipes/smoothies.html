All fruit smoothies

Description

Quick, easy smoothies made entirely with fruit!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup pineapple juice
  • 1 large banana, cut into chunks
  • 1 cup frozen strawberries
  • 1 cup frozen blueberries

Directions

  1. Pour pineapple juice into a blender and add banana, strawberries, and blueberries. Cover and blend until smooth, about 1 minutes. Pour into 2 glasses.
